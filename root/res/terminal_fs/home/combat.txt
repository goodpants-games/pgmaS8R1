== COMBAT TIPS ==

- Don't enter unnecessary fights.
- Be aware of your spacing, positioning, timing, and other enemies in the general vicinity.
- Back off after every melee hit to give yourself space for the next swing.

== CREATURE LOG ==

#04: This creature resembles a blob of flesh with four eyes. Moves slowly yet at an unbreakable efficiency.

#06: These creatures resemble a heart, and serve their entire lives as energy pumps for the facility. They are completely defenseless and unable to move.

#15: This creature resembles a block of metal with eight strings of flesh coming out of its bottom, like an octopus. It is able to fly and hover above ground through unknown means. It uses this flight to dodge melee attacks, but is vulnerable to it when it does its dive-attack.

#31: This creature takes over the bodies of fallen machines. It only moves when not in view. Compared to other creatures, it is invulnerable and does significantly more damage when it attacks. Stay vigilant.
